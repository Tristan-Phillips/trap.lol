The weather is frustrated today.<br>
A day which feels lonely. <br>
Nothing feels quite right. <br>
My chest is tight, my thoughts are spread apart. <br>
Tension sits in my body, my elbows feel compressed, my shoulders are tense. <br>
It is a day to be aware of my body. <br>
To be aware of the tension and to release it. <br>
To be aware of the thoughts and to let them go. <br>
To be aware of the feelings and to let them be. <br>
To be aware of the day and to let it pass. <br>
I am not able to focus on anything. <br>
I am not able to concentrate.<br>
<br>

Something feels wrong. <br>
I cant quite explain it. <br>
The notion of normal feels out of place. <br>
There is an intense desire to withdrawl from it all. <br>
Is there something I refuse to deal with? <br>
Am I hiding from something? <br>
Is something coming for me? <br>
Or is it the other way around? <br>
<br>

I feel like I am standing on the edge of a cliff and about to fall and about to hit the ground, I am about to die, I am about to be forgotten, I am about to be alone.
