Is it possible to die before your death?<br>
And I don't mean the classical, no-feelings die, like we all have.<br>
I mean to die and then to live life.<br>
To live as though you have already died.<br>
To live life knowing you will one day not be able to look back to today, but moments before then you will.<br>
To live life as though you are talking to that you in the future, and telling them, everything is going to be okay.<br>
That you have lived a life, the life you could live, and that there is peace when looking back towards the end.<br>
To have stillness in the mind. Acceptance. Calming of fear.<br>
Living life.<br>
We are not perfect, our lives can not be perfect, our execution can not be perfect, and the timing of our death can not be perfect.<br>
We must live as though we can live a life we aught not have time to live.<br>
I must live my life, the life I have now.<br>
The only life I know myself to have.<br>
