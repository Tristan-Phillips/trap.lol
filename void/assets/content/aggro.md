Sometimes a person gets mad.<br>
Mad for no reason at all.<br>
An innocents voice, brings out the worst in you.<br>
<br>
A person unknowingly destroying real estate within your mind.<br>
You don't know why, and that makes the situation even worse for yourself.<br>
An impulsive panic, upbringing deep trauma, perhaps unrelated to the victim.<br>
<br>
Why can one person make you feel this way, why can you treat an individual with so much injustice.<br>
To not be in control of your emotions, to allow them to spike, possibly extend into the lives around you.<br>
Knowing very well you are in the wrong.<br>
Yet not being able to change it.<br>
<br>
I don't know what to do, it angers me to feel anger.<br>
Compounding its effects.<br>
<br>
If only I would hold my hand before my mouth, and let my emotions get released elsewhere.<br>
<br>
Hate gets brought up because of this lack of control.<br>
Only making the emotion stronger.<br>
